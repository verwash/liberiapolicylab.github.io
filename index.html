Reimagining Liberia's development with ideas, evidence, and action.

Liberia Policy Lab is a youth‑led, boutique policy lab and think tank that brings together young leaders, academics, policymakers, and citizens to co‑create data‑driven solutions across climate & environment, the economy, education, health & wellbeing, employment, governance, and public–private collaboration.

Research & Analysis
Rigorous, evidence-based research to inform policy decisions and drive meaningful change.
Policy Innovation
Creative policy design that addresses Liberia's unique challenges with innovative solutions.
Civic Engagement
Building bridges between citizens, leaders, and institutions for collaborative governance.
Focus Areas
Climate & Environment Economic Development Education Health & Wellbeing Employment Governance Public-Private Partnerships
