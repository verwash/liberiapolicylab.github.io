WHAT WE DO

Turning Evidence into Public Impact

We blend rigorous research, creative policy design, and civic engagement to deliver practical solutions for Liberia.

Research & Analysis

We conduct independent, data-driven research to inform decisions. Our work includes policy briefs, datasets, and evaluations.

  • Quantitative & mixed-methods studies
  • Open data & reproducible analysis
  • Impact evaluations and monitoring

Policy Innovation

We co-design policies and tools with government, civil society, and communities—tailored to local realities.

  • Prototyping and policy pilots
  • Decision-support tools and dashboards
  • Implementation playbooks

Civic Engagement

We bridge citizens and institutions through dialogue, training, and accessible communication.

  • Community consultations & town halls
  • Training for youth & public servants
  • Public-facing explainers & podcasts
Propose a collaboration
We transform groundbreaking research findings into tools for reforms and use data to shape decisions and policy outcomes.
