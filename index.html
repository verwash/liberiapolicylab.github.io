Liberia Policy Lab is a youth‑led, boutique policy lab and think tank that brings together young leaders, academics, policymakers, and citizens to co‑create data‑driven solutions. We combine rigorous research, policy design, and civic engagement across climate & environment, the economy, education, health & wellbeing, employment, governance, and public–private collaboration.
