ABOUT US

Who We Are

Liberia Policy Lab is a youth-led, boutique policy lab and think tank that brings together young leaders, academics, policymakers, and citizens to co-create data-driven solutions to Liberia's development challenges.

Mission

Reimagine Liberia's development through ideas, evidence, and action—transforming research into policy tools that drive sustainable reforms.

Approach

We blend rigorous research, policy innovation, and civic engagement to deliver practical, scalable solutions for public impact.

Values

Integrity, inclusivity, and evidence-based decision-making—centered on Liberia's people and future generations.

Partner With Us
We transform groundbreaking research findings into tools for reforms and use data to shape decisions and policy outcomes.
